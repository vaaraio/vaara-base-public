Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026

Who we are

Vaara is a signal intelligence service operated as a sole proprietorship based in Finland. For questions about this policy, contact us at hello@vaara.io.

What data we collect

Public demo visitors

When you visit vaara.io or use the public demo, we collect only standard server access logs: IP address, browser user-agent, pages visited, and timestamps. We do not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising cookies.

Pilot access users

If you request pilot access, we collect the information you provide in the request form (name, email, protocol affiliation). This is used solely to evaluate and manage pilot access.

API consumers

API requests are logged for operational monitoring (rate limiting, uptime, debugging). Logs contain request metadata (endpoint, timestamp, response code) and are retained for up to 90 days.

What we do not collect

  • Wallet addresses or on-chain identity
  • Trading activity or portfolio data
  • Personal financial information
  • Data from third-party trackers or ad networks

Cookies

We use a single functional cookie (base_access) to maintain pilot access sessions. This cookie is strictly necessary for authenticated access and contains no personal data. We do not use analytics or advertising cookies.

Data processing and storage

Data is processed on servers located in Finland and the European Union. We do not transfer personal data outside the EU/EEA. Server logs and operational data are retained for up to 90 days and then deleted.

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties. Server infrastructure providers may process data as part of hosting services, subject to EU data protection regulations.

Your rights (GDPR)

As we operate from Finland, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies. You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi)

To exercise these rights, email hello@vaara.io.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our service evolves. Material changes will be noted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.
