How it works

Multiple layers,
one judgment call.

Vaara ingests live market and on-chain data, resolves it into a coherent market state, then applies cost-aware filtering before any action recommendation reaches the surface.

Ingest
Live market data

Price data, on-chain swap and order flow signals direct from Base L2, yield market data, cross-market indicators, and protocol TVL. Continuous ingestion from multiple sources, not periodic snapshots.

Resolve
Market state + confidence

Multi-timeframe momentum scoring, trend alignment, and breakout state detection. The system knows whether it's in a trend, range, or transition, and how confident it is in that assessment.

Filter
Cost-aware action selection

The stateful selector applies a switching cost penalty. It won't change position unless the expected advantage exceeds the cost of switching. Every suppressed switch is logged with its cost estimate.
